Sorrel

Recipe By: Laura Job

About This Drink

Sorrel drink is made from the petals of a species of Hibiscus, known as Hibiscus sabdariffa (Roselle). It can be used fresh, frozen, or dried, but it is mostly sold in dried form.

Sorrel Health Benefits

Sorrel has very high concentrations of fiber and vitamin C. It includes other nutrients such as vitamin A, vitamin B6, iron, calcium, potassium, and magnesium. So, this drink while being tasty can have many health benefits, it all depends on how much sugar you add.

Different Ways Sorrel Is Made

Different islands give sorrel their own spin, varying the spices and other ingredients. Some people add ginger ale to the mix. Wine, rum or other alcohols can be used for the optional buzzy kick. Some people let the sorrel sit for 1-3 days before drinking it. However, my family drinks it the same day made.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dried sorrel
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 ounces of ginger
  • 1 Bael leaf
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 5 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons Orange Bitters by Angostura

Directions

  1. In a pot, bring 5 cups of water to a boil. Add the sorrel cinnamon stick, barl leaf, cloves and ginger.
  2. Stir continuously while the mixture boils for 15 minutes.
  3. Strain the sorrel drink into a glass jar and put the sorrel leaves aside for later to reuse or throw them away
  4. After draining the sorrel add the 2 cups of sugar to the drink and stir.
  5. Taste for strength and sweetness. If it is too strong, add water or if too tart add more sugar
  6. ENJOY!!

why this is my favorite recipe!!!

My family is from Trinidad and Tobago, so I was introduced to this drink as a little girl as a tradition. We usually drink it only on special occasions such as Christmas and birthdays. This is my favorite recipe because of how simple it is to make yet how flavorful it is. It's sweet, bitter, tangy and has a hint of spice all at once. I love that I can taste all the flavors separately, all the ingredients complement each other so well. I get excited when I get to share this amazing drink on special occasions with my friends. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do!
